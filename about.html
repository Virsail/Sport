What is i_Grocer?

I~Grocer is a locally owned grocer store in Kenya. It is incorporated under Kenyan Laws .
It is one of the leading names in the retail industry offering the best grocer deals and the freshest grocer products. I~Grocer has its Head-office in Nairobi
Despite the severe economic environment and intense competition in the industry , i_Grocer has continued to grow to achieve greater strides.
